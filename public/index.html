How to book a study room

  1. Choose a Study Room
  2. Click on the calendar
  3. Set a password, Enter your name and school ID, Pick a time you wish to book
    4. *You have to fill out the required field to confirm your reservation*
  4. The room you selected will now appear in the Location field

To delete your schedule

  1. Click on the event you want to change
  2. Enter your password
  3. The event you selected will now disappear

To change your schedule

  1. Drag the event you want to change
    2. * you can scroll down to extend the end time *
  2. Enter your password
  3. The event you selected will now change
